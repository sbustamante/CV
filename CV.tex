\cvitem{\small\textbf{Thesis}}
	{``The place of the Milky Way and Andromeda in the cosmic web''.}
	
\cvitem{\small\textbf{Description}}
	{This study assesses the representation of vertical integral of moisture flux divergence field (MFD) from ERA Interim reanalysis over
northern South America, focused on Colombian. Temporal and spatial variability MFD field are explored
in connection with other hydrological variables 
and global climate phenomena
of regional impact, the MFD variability were
 evaluated through an EOF analysis whose main
components are associated in
 order with: the North Tropical Atlantic and the El Niño Southern Oscillation.}
